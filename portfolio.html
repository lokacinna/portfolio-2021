My Portfolio


Below, you’ll find a variety of work samples that I feel in some way embody my skill set. They may be complete and polished, or partially finished if I believe they’re a good example anyway. They begin with websites, then infographics, print materials, writing samples, and finally video.

Websites

CITAP Digital Politics

This website hosts three – soon to be four – academic reports on digital communications in the US political sphere. I helped choose a CMS, worked with a Wordpress theme, created a layout and format, and solidified color design.

Classics Department

This website serves the Classics majors – undergraduate and graduate – as well as professors. I helped update the theme on the UNC system and created a variety of engaging pages, such as the “Departmental History” and Staff layouts.

Azoria Project

This was originally HTML and CSS only – I transferred it to the UNC system for ease of use and restructured it to the project leader’s specifications. It was formulated for professional academic reports and meant to be minimalistic.
